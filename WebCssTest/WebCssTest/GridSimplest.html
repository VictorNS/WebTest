The hardest part about grids is gutters.
So far we've made our grid flexible by using percentages for widths.
We could make the math all complicated and use percentages for gutters as well, but personally
I don't like percentage gutters anyway, I like fixed pixel size gutters.
Plus, we're trying to keep too much thinking out of this.
